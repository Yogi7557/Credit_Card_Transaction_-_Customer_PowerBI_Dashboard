CREDIT CARD DASHBOARD
Project Objectives and Insights
--------------------------------------------------

PROJECT OBJECTIVE
To develop a comprehensive credit card weekly dashboard that provides real-time insights into key performance metrics and trends, enabling stakeholders to monitor and analyze credit card operations effectively.

--------------------------------------------------

EXECUTIVE INSIGHTS – CREDIT CARD ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

• Total Revenue: 55.3M with 7.8M interest and 655K transactions, showing strong usage.
• Blue card segment generates the highest revenue, indicating most customers are in entry-level tiers.
• Swipe transactions dominate, while online transactions are comparatively low.
• Age group 40–50 and business professionals contribute the most revenue, making them key customer segments.
• Major spending categories include Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, and Grocery, showing focus on essential expenses.
• High-income and graduate customers generate higher revenue.
• Revenue is concentrated in a few top-performing states, suggesting opportunities to expand in other regions.
• Customer satisfaction is moderate, indicating scope for improvement through better rewards and services.

--------------------------------------------------

CONCLUSION

The business is performing well, with strong transaction volume and revenue, but growth can be achieved by:
• Promoting premium cards
• Increasing digital payments
• Targeting high-value customer segments
